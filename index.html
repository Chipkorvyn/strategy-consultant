Strategy Consultant a.k.a Worker

Problem. Research. Analysis. Deliverable.

This plugin handles 80 to 90% of the analytical workload in a strategy engagement: research, evidence validation, structured synthesis, and report drafting. You focus on the 10 to 20% that creates unique value: client relationship judgment, proprietary insight, and final recommendations.

Built for management consultants, corporate strategy teams, corporate development, and independent advisors.

How you'll work with it

1
Describe the business problem
Asks for your documents, sharpens the question, builds a deliverable blueprint.
2
Three agents research it independently
Two analysts on tailored angles. A third validates for consistency and source quality.
3
Everything gets pressure-tested
Triangulation, counter-arguments, math checks, bias scanning, altitude verification.
4
You get a structured working draft
Executive-grade Word document. Every claim sourced. Research notes appendix included.

Get started

Download and upload to Cowork
  1. Download and unzip the plugin folder
  2. Open the Claude desktop app, switch to Cowork
  3. Click Customize, then Browse plugins, then Upload plugin
  4. Select the unzipped folder
Download plugin
Using the plugin

Once installed, the plugin activates automatically when Cowork recognises a relevant task. You can also ask Cowork to use it directly, for example: "Use the Strategy Consultant plugin to analyse..."

This plugin is continuously improved based on real consulting engagements.
