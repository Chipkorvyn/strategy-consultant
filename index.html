Claude Cowork Plugin · Open Source

Strategy Consultant

Research, validation, synthesis, and an executive-grade report. From a business problem to a client-ready working draft.

How you'll work with it

1
Describe the business problem
The plugin sharpens it into a decision-oriented question, asks for your documents (client transcripts, expert interviews, internal data, industry reports) and builds a deliverable blueprint before any research begins.
2
Three agents research it independently
Two analysts work in parallel on dynamically generated angles tailored to your specific question, not generic categories. A third cross-checks both for consistency, source quality, and conflicts with your expert data.
3
Everything gets pressure-tested
Mandatory sense-check: triangulation, counter-arguments, math checks, bias scanning, and answer altitude verification. Every finding must operate at the level of specificity your client actually asked for.
4
You get a structured working draft
An executive-grade Word document with a storyline built around the logic of the answer, every claim traced to its source, and a research notes appendix for verification.

This plugin handles 80 to 90% of the analytical workload in a strategy engagement: research, evidence validation, structured synthesis, and report drafting. The consultant focuses on the 10 to 20% that creates unique value: client relationship judgment, proprietary insight, and final recommendations.

All outputs are high-quality working drafts for expert review, not finished deliverables.

Who it's for

Management Consultants

Accelerate the research-to-deliverable cycle at strategy and generalist firms.

Corporate Strategy Teams

In-house strategists running internal engagements for the C-suite.

Corporate Development / M&A

Teams scoping market entries, acquisitions, and competitive landscapes.

Independent Advisors

Solo or small-team consultants who need the analytical depth of a larger team.

Get started

Download and upload to Cowork
  1. Download the plugin folder
  2. Open the Claude desktop app → switch to Cowork
  3. Click Customize → Browse plugins → Upload plugin
  4. Select the downloaded folder
Download plugin
Or install via command line
  1. Run claude plugin marketplace add Chipkorvyn/strategy-consultant
  2. Then claude plugin install strategy-consultant
